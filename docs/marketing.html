🔥

Disappearing Notes

Every note has a countdown. Choose 5 minutes, 1 hour, 24 hours, 3 days, or 7 days. When the timer hits zero, it's gone forever. No recovery. No traces.

⏳

Live Countdown

Watch the time tick down in real time. A progress bar shifts from gold to bronze to flame red as it approaches zero. You'll always know exactly how much time remains.

📜

Burn on Demand

Can't wait for the timer? Tap the flame icon to destroy a note instantly. Once burned, it can never be recovered.

🏛

Classical Design

Serif typography, parchment textures, gold accents, and daily quotes from Plato. Your fleeting thoughts deserve a beautiful stage.

🔒

Completely Offline

No accounts. No cloud. No internet required. Zero network requests. Your notes exist on your device and nowhere else.

100% Open Source

Plato is free, open source, and licensed under MIT. No ads, no in-app purchases, no tracking. The entire codebase is on GitHub for anyone to inspect, fork, and contribute to.
